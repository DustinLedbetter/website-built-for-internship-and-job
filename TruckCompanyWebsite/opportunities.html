Prospective Applicants,

    We are a constantly growing transportation company located in Knoxville, TN. We offer a friendly and enjoyable work enviroment. A new driver can expect a competitive pay,  Health Insurance, benefits, and great home time. As we continue to grow, we will be looking to add more trucks and trailers to our current fleet. As a result, we will need more drivers to drive those trucks. We are looking for drivers who have good driving records and good work habits. No short cuts are accepted here; We expect our drivers to take care of their equipment as if it were their own. Must have a valid CDL and minimum of 3 years experience, U.S. Mail hauling experience is a plus.

Looking for these types of drivers:
- OTR Drivers
- Solo and Team drivers

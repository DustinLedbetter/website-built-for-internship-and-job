Repair Service Vehicle:

We here at Truck Company have a full service mobile repair truck. Our trucks are ready to go so that we can bring the supplies to your vehicle and have it repaired as quickly as possible. Call us anytime whether the sun is shining or the moon is glowing. We would be more than happy to help you get back on the road and meeting your deadlines.
Roadside repair services Truck Company offer include:
- Jump starts and out of fuel
- Battery replacement
- Tire replacement and repair
- Air Brake replacement and repair
- Complete truck and trailer mechanical diagnosis and repair
- Feel free to inquire about any other Mechanical or Other issues we may offer for roadside assistance
 
Some repairs that cannot be fixed on the side of the road but can be worked on at our yard include:
- Oil changes
- Complete trailer/van body and roof repair
- Complete metal and aluminum fabrication
- Feel free to inquire about any other Mechanical or Other issues we may offer for non-roadside assistance
 

Pricing:

Pricing is dependant on several factors. The first is the price of work per hour, which includes a minimum charge for 2 hours of repair service. Second is the cost per mile driven to get to the site of the repair service. Last is the price of the parts used during the repair service, which will vary depending on the type of repair being done.
Standard Prices:
  - Rate per hour: $75
  - Rate per mile: $2
  - Price of parts: contact for an estimate
Nights and Weekends:
  - Rate per hour: $115
  - Rate per mile: $2
  - Price for parts: same as standard
